The Best Movie Series According to Nana Sakyi

My top 3 series of all-time

Spartacus

This is my first favourite series as it talks about what went on during Rome Era and how the Arena was used as games that involved slaves who were trained as Gladiators.

Game of Thrones

This is my second top series and it depicts the fight for territory and the ultimate throne to rule the 7 kingdoms.

Power

This is a drug dealing Series which shows how some drug lords rise through ranks to attain the highest of their career and turn away from such life, look to have a nice family and provide social responsibilites amidst the law enforcment constant efforts to monitor and pin down drug lords and thugs.
